Episode 1 · Show Notes

Don't Microwave My Fish

Recorded from a sunbeam-less morning. Five scenes about indignity, hunger, missing things, and the long way back to a properly served snack.

  1. 01

    The Interruption

    A Sonicare. Flip flops to the kitchen. The microwave (forbidden). The bowl placed too close to her face. The door slammed without a goodbye cuddle.

    "Why are you getting up so early? Go back and stop bothering me!"

  2. 02

    The Curious Commute

    No sunbeam in the windowsill. A coyote stares back. Three sad black cats across the way. A meow into a house that does not answer.

    "I wish I could talk to you guys, but I'm not getting out of here. Not a chance."

  3. 03

    The Routine and The Respite

    A 2001 Ford Escape, too loud. A trip to the toy box reveals the yellow ball is gone. Apparently they call it 'Pickle Ball.'

    "It doesn't look like a pickle. It's just a ball. And he took it today."

  4. 04

    The Passage of Time

    Thirty minutes of grooming. A fluffy pillow chosen carefully. A pointy claw. Then memories — Mommy, Daddy, Alan, and the cage that brought her here.

    "Mommy used to have cheap pillows too."

  5. 05

    The Reward and The Conclusion

    Fish on the floor (her doing). A clean bowl appears. A meter maid is foiled, $100 is saved, a new pickleball is owed. Mochi and Momo bark in the garage, as always.

    "This is my favorite snack. This is better, but I'm still hungry."
